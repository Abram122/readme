Webbrical is an e-learning platform that aims to enhance students' learning experience by providing a fun and engaging website design with modern flexibility and functionality. The platform offers a unique PpT system, which stands for Point per Topic, where students earn points based on the topic of their answers. This system encourages students to put in maximum effort and earn certificates from the platform. Webbrical is the future of E-learning.
